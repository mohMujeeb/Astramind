Mount Everest, located in the Himalayas, is the tallest mountain in the world with a height of 8,848 meters (29,029 feet). 
The Amazon River in South America is the largest river by discharge volume of water.

Africa is the second-largest continent in the world. It is home to the Sahara Desert, the largest hot desert on Earth, and the Nile River, the longest river in the world.
